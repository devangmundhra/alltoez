Story of Alltoez

Alltoez began with the observation that kids are getting more engrossed with screens and gadgets than doing social and physical activities. One reason we realized when talking to parents is that its not easy to find interesting activities for the kids. Thats how Alltoez was born, a way to helps parents and kids find activities they love to do.

We believe technology is an enabler, and we want to use it well to help parents and kids. If you have any ideas and comments, share it with us!
